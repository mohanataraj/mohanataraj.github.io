Q) What makes a great user experience for you ?

  • easy to use. (such as signup, signin and sharing results)
    • very little time needed to learn or very intuitive app design required.
    • clear icons
    • interface design
  • minimalistic approach, such as keeping the navigation to just 4 pages etc…
    • smooth navigation or no unnecessary step required.
  • keep track of everything necessary for smoother transition from initial start to making it an habit [very important]
  • clean design and consistent design (colors)
  • classified or grouped acccordingly.
  • not too much notification (leads to user disengagment)
  • more of image based app
    • landing page is news feeds
    • less text content
  • app should be fun to use.
  • MYFITNESS PAL : ability to set goals, target and track progress in weight or percentage.
  • comphrensive : input food, exercis, drinks etc
  • features workout and nothing screams at you.

NOTE: Three major points for great UX :

  • minimalistic approach - to get to the purpose/goal almost instantly. (usually within 3/4 - clicks)
  • easy to use - such as navigation, icons, interface design (classified food & exercise correctly)
  • fun to use - more image/video based, less text, enjoyable contents (quality of content rather than quantity? depends on what drives revenue.)
